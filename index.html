01 — About

Turning data into decisions people actually trust

I'm a data analyst and compliance specialist who likes the unglamorous part of data work: making it correct, consistent, and usable by people who aren't analysts. My favorite problems live at the intersection of engineering, governance, and storytelling — building the pipeline, locking down the quality, and then making the dashboard so clear that leadership can act in a meeting instead of a follow-up.

My path is intentionally cross-disciplinary. A B.Tech in Computer Science gave me the engineering foundation; an M.S. in Business Analytics taught me to tie models to business value; and roles across a national nonprofit, an impact-investing fund, a conservation org, and a PayPal-incubated startup taught me that the same skill — making data trustworthy and legible — creates value everywhere.

Right now I'm in an Agentic AI bootcamp, going hands-on with RAG, agent and tool design, evaluation, and guardrails for production LLM apps — because the next chapter of analytics is conversational, and I want to build it responsibly.
